1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how LibrarEase collects, uses, and protects data from both library admins and library users.

2. Information We Collect

We collect:

  • **From Library Admins:** Name, email, library details, books added, and borrowing records.
  • **From Library Users:** Name, email, membership details, borrowing history, and interactions with the platform.
  • **System Data:** Device logs, IP addresses, and platform usage analytics.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve library management services.
  • To track and facilitate book borrowings and returns.
  • To prevent fraud, security risks, and unauthorized access.
  • To communicate important updates or policy changes.

4. Data Sharing & Third Parties

Library Admins can only access their own members’ borrowing records.

We do not sell or rent user data.

We may share data with third-party service providers for security, analytics, or legal compliance.

5. Data Security & Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary for service operations or legal compliance.

No system is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute protection against unauthorized access.

  • Certain data (e.g., borrowing records) may be retained for legal or security reasons.
